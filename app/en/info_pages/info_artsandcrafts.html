Arts & Crafts

In Turku there are plenty of possibilities to learn arts and crafts and to attend courses of photography, drawing or writing. Just pick the hobby you enjoy most!

Unfortunately the websites below are only available in Finnish, but you can get contact details from their pages (yhteystiedot) and contact the personnel for mor information.

Turun kuvataidekoulu offers many kinds of art and craft courses for all.

Also Manillan kuvataidekeskus organizes art courses for all interested in arts.

Setlementti Aurala is an adult education center that offers courses related to arts, crafts, photography and creative expression.

Paasikivi-opisto institute offers courses related to photography, drawing and creative writing etc.

Turun Sunnuntaimaalarit association offers many kinds of art courses, located at Barkerin Ateljeet building.

At Vimma, you can come and do crafts, music and media, participate in events, or just visit to feel the vibes.

Turun Taitokeskus organizes craft courses.

Työväen Sivistysliiton Turun opintojärjestö ry organizes different kind of courses and cultural events open to all.
