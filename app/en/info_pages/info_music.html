Music

You have many possibilities to practice music as a hobby in Turku. There are many places, bands and choirs where you can sing or play musical instruments. Just choose the most suitable one for you below.

If you know how to play an instrument, there are some student bands in Turku; you are more than welcome to join.

If you enjoy singing you can join one of the student choirs. For more information, contact the choirs’ offices.

At the University of Turku there are three student choirs, one general, called Turun yliopiston kuoro, one for female (mostly medical) students only - Arrhytmics, and one from The Turku School of Economics called Cantus Mercurialis.

Turun yliopiston kuoro (TYY’s choir) contact email: raati@yliopistonkuoro.fi
Arrhytmics contact email: arrhythmicskuoro@gmail.com
Cantus Mercurialis contact email: puheenjohtaja@cantusmercurialis.fi

At Åbo Akademi University there are two choirs: Brahe Djäknar for male singers and Florakören for female singers.

Brahe Djäknar contact email: styrelsen@brahedjaknar.fi
Florakören contact email: flora@abo.fi

In Turku there are many possibilities to take lessons in playing a musical instrument or singing. Check out the places and choose the most suitable one for you! Unfortunately most of the web pages are only in Finnish, but please find the contact information (yhteystiedot) from their pages and contact them directly for more information.
