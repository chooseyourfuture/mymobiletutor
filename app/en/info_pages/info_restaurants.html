Restaurants

Turku has a lively restaurant culture that offers something for everyone. Whether you're looking for fine dining or simple street food, you can find your new favourite restaurant here!

Especially the Aurajoki riverside is full of lovely restaurants, which offer both lunch and dinner. Many old river boats have been transformed as restaurants and bars and are great places especially in summer times. Kaskis has been repeatedly voted as the best restaurant in Turku but be aware of the long waiting time. Other local favorites are for example (some pages unfortunately only in Finnish) Di Trevi, Kuori (website in Finnish), Niska, Pinella, Tintå and Smör.

In addition there are also many good ethnic restaurants, such as Delhi Darbar, Kawaii, Maneerat and Yangtze. In fact many ethnic restaurants are right on Hämeenkatu near the university.

The prices for lunch are usually around 10€. Dinner prices vary, yet main courses are often around 17-30€ in good restaurants. It is not common to leave a tip in restaurants unless you receive excellent service. Many smaller restaurants require advance booking especially in holidays and weekends. During the week it is quite easy to find free seats.
