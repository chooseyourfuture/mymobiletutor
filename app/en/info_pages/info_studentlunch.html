Student Lunches

In Finland, eating out is fairly expensive, yet various restaurants have nice lunch options and deals. However, students can eat subsidised student meals at the student cafeterias. The state subsidises a part of the cost of each student lunch and the price is 2,60€. This type of lunch usually includes: one main course with side dish, glass of drink, salad and bread. Some cafeterias also include Deli-dishes in their menus which usually cost around 3,50-6,00€ for students.

Lunch menus of all student cafeterias close by can be found here (only in Finnish). Unica, which has 11 restaurants near the university, provide their menus also in English.

In order to get student lunches, you need a Finnish Student Card. Without the student card the price of the lunch in the same cafeterias is around 4,50-6,50€.
